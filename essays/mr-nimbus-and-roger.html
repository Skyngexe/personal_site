Mr Nimbus and Roger

When it comes to digital persona, most named their instagram account after their own name and picked their picture as the icon, which is perfectly normal and the common way of associating your extension of identity on the online space. It used to be the same case for me - I even named my instagram account after some cringy attention-grabbing name because I thought it’s cool. Looking back, such action was not completely authentic and was a cry to be noticed (as an attention-seeking teenager who was still exploring his identity).

After I got into uni, I renamed it to Mr Nimbus, a character from Rick and Morty as the nemesis of Rick, and lately I updated my icon to Roger from American Dad. When people ask for my instagram account, they usually follow up with the question: Why Mr Nimbus? My typical answer is just a smirk.

But I know it is a solidification of my identity, that publicly I laugh at absurdity of life to offset the burden of life, that internally I write to confess my complex feelings and beliefs to my inner circles, for they are invited to stare deep in my soul (voluntarily or involuntarily) and witness my coming. Mr Nimbus is the manifestation of my preposterousness as my protective shield, while Roger is my polymorphic identity, that I am not bounded by rules but the limit of my imagination.
